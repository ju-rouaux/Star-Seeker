Calculate the intersection of a rectangle and line segment.

This function is used to clip a line segment to a rectangle. A line segment contained entirely within the rectangle or that does not intersect will remain unchanged. A line segment that crosses the rectangle at either or both ends will be clipped to the boundary of the rectangle and the new coordinates saved in {\ttfamily X1}, {\ttfamily Y1}, {\ttfamily X2}, and/or {\ttfamily Y2} as necessary.

\hline
{\em Y1} & a pointer to the starting Y-\/coordinate of the line \\
\hline
{\em X2} & a pointer to the ending X-\/coordinate of the line \\
\hline
{\em Y2} & a pointer to the ending Y-\/coordinate of the line \\
\hline
\end{DoxyParams}
